What Is Algorithmic Trading?

Algorithmic trading stands for trading stocks, currency, and other commodities, where a computer program or algorithm follows a predefined set of directions to execute a trade. The main point is to give users the ability to trade as often and as fast as a bot. In other words, with vision, you can generate profits in quantities that are practically impossible for a human trader.

Algorithmic trading is also known as automated trading, black-box trading, or simply algo-trading for short. The name comes from the fact that an algorithm is effectively a step-by-step process used to accomplish a specific task. The main aspect is always that the system uses a defined set of instructions users can set up on their own. These instructions are typically based on timing, quantity, price, or any other mathematical model. With this form of trading, you can effectively eliminate mistakes often made by humans. You can also eliminate emotions, which rarely have a place in trading but are hard to turn off for most of us.

With algorithmic trading, all you need to do is give instructions in advance when you’re clear about what you want to achieve. Afterward, you can simply let the app do its magic. With Vision, trading becomes much easier. The charts we provide are detailed and easy to follow, and they’ll give you all the information you need to make vision as successful as possible. We give you the insights and you make the trades.
